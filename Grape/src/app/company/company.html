Our Company History

Grape was founded in 2020 with a simple vision: to create revolutionary technology products that would change how people interact with devices. What started in a small garage has grown into an innovative company that sets new industry standards. Over the years, we've expanded globally, built an incredible team of passionate individuals, and launched groundbreaking products including our flagship Grape 17 series. Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, incorporating cutting-edge AI and machine learning technologies. Today, Grape stands as a leader in the tech industry, committed to delivering quality, efficiency, and fair pricing to customers worldwide. We continue to push boundaries and shape the future of technology, staying true to our core values that have guided us from the very beginning.
